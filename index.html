English Test: Judging Sentences

In this first task you will read English sentences and determine if they sound grammatical to you. In other words, do you think it would sound odd for your friends to say this to you, or fine?

Click Start Questions when you are ready.

Start Questions

Judging Sentences

1. Which of the following continuations is/are acceptable? Select multiple options if they are correct.

I was talking to Susan about John. She said...

...she understood why he wanted some time alone this week.

...she understood why he wanted any time alone this week.

...she understood why did he want some time alone this week.

...she understood why he wants some time alone this week.
